Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?
Absolutely. IPLoop only routes publicly available web requests through your connection. We never access your personal data, files, or browsing history. All traffic is encrypted.
Will it slow down my internet?
No. IPLoop uses only idle bandwidth ‚Äî typically less than 5% of your connection. You won't notice any difference in your browsing or streaming.
How do credits work?
Every GB of bandwidth you share earns approximately 100 credits. Each proxy request costs about 1 credit. Credits are calculated automatically and added to your balance in real-time.
How do I get free VPN?
Simply keep at least one device actively sharing bandwidth. As long as you're contributing, your VPN access stays active ‚Äî no extra cost.
What's the multi-device bonus?
Run IPLoop on 3 or more devices simultaneously and earn 2x credits on all bandwidth shared. Stack it with the 24-hour uptime bonus (1.5x) and rare geo bonus (up to 3x) for maximum earnings.
Which platforms are supported?
Mac, Linux, Windows, Docker, Android, Fire TV, and Android TV. We're adding iOS support soon.
Is there a minimum to cash out?
No minimum. Your credits are immediately usable for proxy requests or VPN access. No waiting, no thresholds.
