Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

1. Who We Are

IPLoop is a proxy network service operated from Israel. Contact: privacy@iploop.io

2. What Data We Collect

  • IP address — to identify your node's location and route traffic
  • Country — derived from IP for geographic routing
  • Uptime hours — to calculate credit earnings
  • Credits balance — earned and spent credits
  • Node token — unique identifier for your node
  • Connection metadata — timestamps, connection status, basic device info (OS, architecture)

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Browsing history or URLs accessed through the proxy
  • Personal data (name, email, phone) unless you provide it
  • Content of proxied traffic
  • Keystroke data or screen recordings
  • Contacts, photos, or files on your device

4. How Proxy Traffic Works

IPLoop routes HTTPS traffic through participating nodes. All proxy connections use TLS encryption. We do not log, inspect, or store the content of proxied requests. Traffic passes through your node but is encrypted end-to-end between the proxy client and the destination server.

5. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for session management and analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party trackers. The IPLoop node software does not use cookies.

6. Data Retention

  • Connection logs: 30 days, then deleted
  • Credit records: Retained while your account is active
  • Bandwidth statistics: Aggregated, retained for 90 days

7. GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing of your personal data. Contact privacy@iploop.io to exercise these rights. We will respond within 30 days.

8. CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. Contact privacy@iploop.io for requests.

9. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews to protect your data.

10. Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing. We may share aggregated, anonymized statistics with proxy customers (e.g., node counts by country).

11. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via our website or dashboard.

12. Contact
