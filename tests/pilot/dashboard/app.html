Key Trends

📈 Residential proxy demand surging

Enterprise customers shifting from datacenter to residential IPs for better detection avoidance. +30% YoY demand.

📱 Mobile SDK monetization growing

App developers increasingly adding proxy SDKs as monetization alternative to ads. Avg RPM: $0.02-0.10/device/day.

🔒 Compliance requirements tightening

Google Play and Apple App Store increasing scrutiny on VPN/proxy SDKs. Transparency requirements growing.

🌍 Geo-specific demand rising

High demand for IPs in: US, UK, DE, JP, BR. Premium pricing for rare geolocations.

Opportunities

✅ Smart TV proxy network

No major competitors in Smart TV proxy space. Our Weathero app on Samsung/LG TVs could provide unique residential IPs.

✅ White-label SDK for small app devs

Most SDKs target large apps. Opportunity to provide easy-to-integrate SDK for indie developers.

⚡ Multi-hop proxy chains

Premium feature: chain proxies through multiple residential nodes. Few competitors offer this.

💡 API-first proxy management

Developer-focused API with better DX than competitors. Target: DevOps teams and SaaS platforms.
